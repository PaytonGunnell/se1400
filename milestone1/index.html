Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

The website will contain projects that I have worked on or are currently working on. It will also have any certifications I have that a recruiter would like to see. It will contain a a page for my resume and an about me section. It will also have a section for my contact information.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

The text will come from me and I will use fonts available for free. Any of the images will either come from me or stock images.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users who are interested in my portfolio will care because it will give them valuable imformation about me and my skills in an easy to scan way.

Why should a site be buit for this?

I think its important to build a website for this because having a well put together website will in and of itself show skill and effort put in. It is also something that I can build upon for years to come, improving it and adding more content as my career progresses.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I want the build the site so that users going through it are interested in it and enjoy going through some of the projects that I put on it.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to have a recruiter go through the site and then contact me.
